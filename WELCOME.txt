GUBA TEXT EDITOR MANUAL

Keybindings:
    Move around:    Use the cursor keys
    Delete text:    Press BACKSPACE
    Insert line:    Press RETURN
    Save file:      Press F1
    Go to BOL:      Press HOME
    Go to EOL:      Press END
    Page Up:        Press PAGEUP
    Page Down:      Press PAGEDOWN

Have fun :)
